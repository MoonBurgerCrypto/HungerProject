What is Starvation?

Starvation is a severe deficiency in caloric energy, nutrient, and vitamin intake. It is the most extreme form of malnutrition and can result in death if untreated. Millions of people around the world suffer from this crisis every day.

How You Can Help

You can start by learning more on this site: explore the causes, read the facts, and get involved in solutions. Even small actions make a difference.
