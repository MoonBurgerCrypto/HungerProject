What is Starvation?

Starvation is a severe lack of food and nutrients that leads to suffering and death. Millions of people, especially children, face hunger every single day due to poverty, war, and inequality.

Why It Matters

Hunger is not just about food — it affects education, health, and future generations. Ending hunger means creating real opportunities for people to thrive.

Key Statistics

  • Over 800 million people suffer from chronic hunger worldwide.
  • Every 10 seconds, a child dies from hunger-related causes.
  • 1 in 9 people globally go to bed hungry each night.
  • Most hunger-related deaths occur in developing countries.
